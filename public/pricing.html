Quick questions
Can I cancel at any time? ›
Yes — no contracts, no catch. Cancel from your account settings any time and you won't be charged again. Monthly subscribers keep access until the end of their billing period.
Is my data safe? ›
Your data is stored securely and never sold to anyone. Payments are handled by Stripe — we never see your card details. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
Does TerryBot replace my accountant? ›
TerryBot gives you estimates and helps you stay organised — but it's not a replacement for professional tax advice. Always check with HMRC or a qualified accountant before filing your Self Assessment.
Can I switch from monthly to annual? ›
Absolutely — upgrade to annual any time from your account settings and we'll credit the remaining days on your monthly plan against the annual price.
Why only 1 free invoice and 1 free expense? ›
We want you to try TerryBot properly before committing — but to keep it fair for paying members, the free tier is limited to one of each. Free accounts are verified by email and mobile so everyone gets one fair try.
TerryBot is not a regulated financial service. Tax estimates are for guidance only.
